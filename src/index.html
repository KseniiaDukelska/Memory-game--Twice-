Twice is a Kpop girl group formed in 2015 by JYP Entertainment.

The group consists of nine members: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. Twice has gained widespread popularity and critical acclaim for their catchy pop songs, intricate choreography, and bubbly personalities. The group has released several successful albums and singles, including "Fancy," "Feel Special," and "I Can't Stop Me." Twice has won numerous awards and nominations, including several Mnet Asian Music Awards and Seoul Music Awards. They have also gained a massive following on social media, with millions of fans worldwide. Twice is known for their strong vocal performances, captivating dance routines, and colorful music videos, and they continue to be one of the most popular Kpop groups of all time.
